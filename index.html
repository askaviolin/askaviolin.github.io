Welcome!

Harmonie Hall is a historic building in Manayunk, Philadelphia, built in 1891 as a German social lounge and dance hall. Today, it is a venue and community event space.

Are you interested in hosting a house concert, art show, film showing, etc. at Harmonie Hall? Is there a band you think we should check out? Please let us know!

If you'd like to hear about our events directly, the best way is to sign up for our email list. You can also follow us on instagram or facebook
