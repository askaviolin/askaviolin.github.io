Ora Cogan, Anni Rossi, and Open Hand

Friday, May 19, 2017, Doors 8pm, Music 9pm

Ora Cogan's versatile, haunted voice opens up a mystical reality; hallowed, forlorn and full of promise. Ora combines the intricate guitar picking of americana with grunge and psychedelic dreamscapes, drawing comparisons to 70's folk legend Karen Dalton and sharing the stage with the likes of Chelsea Wolfe, Grouper & Hope Sandoval while touring extensively throughout North America & Europe.


Ora's 6th studio album, Shadowland, was released on Hidden City Records and Beacon Sound in 2016. A co-production with Trish Klein from acclaimed alt-folk group The Be Good Tanyas, Shadowland sees Ora's stunning voice set in an intriguing romantic dreamscape of electric guitar, vintage keyboards, and hypnotic drums. Ora will be returning to share her mournful magic here in Philly for her third time along her 2017 tour. We are eagerly awaiting the release of her new album, Crickets, recently recorded in Philly with Tom Deis of Uni Ika Aia and Via Audio.

Anni Rossi combines elements of indie pop with her classically trained background with viola.

https://soundcloud.com/anni_rossi


Open Hand is Anya Rose of Ants on a Log and Nothing Wrong, Mike Stevens of PhillyBloco, Peter Oswald of Snagwing and My Son Bison, and Joel Price, creating a complex mixture of intense Brazilian percussion, strings, and vocal harmonies.
