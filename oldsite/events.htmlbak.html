Uni Ika Ai, Falsa, and Other Colors

Saturday, December 3, 2016, 8:00pm

Uni Ika Ai are a Brooklyn-based group, with music full of surreal, underwater imagery. They've been getting all kinds of good press lately, and we're excited to host them.


Falsa is a new project fronted by Umer Piracha, classically trained, Qawwali singer. He shares with us ancient Sufi and Urdu songs, as well as original songs.


Other Colors is a lo-fi, indie rock band from Baltimore, MD, inspired by the powerlessness of the city in the face a heavy blizzard.
