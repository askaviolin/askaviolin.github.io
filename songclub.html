Tuesday Songwriting Club

Led by Ilan Isakov

Tuesday evenings at Harmonie Hall
6:00pm - Arrival, tea and snacks
6:30pm - Writing
8:00pm - Sharing

The idea is to write an entire song in one session using timed, guided writing exercises. Those who would like to share their fresh songs are encouraged.

Bring: notebook, pen, an instrument, snacks if you wish
